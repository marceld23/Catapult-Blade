# Code of Conduct

This is a small, friendly hobby project — a game made by a kid. Please keep it that way.

## Be kind
- Be respectful, patient and welcoming. Assume good intent.
- No harassment, insults, discrimination or hostile behaviour.
- Keep feedback constructive and remember a 10‑year‑old made the game.

## Scope
Applies to issues, pull requests, discussions and any other project space.

## Reporting
If someone behaves badly, please open an issue or contact the repository owner
([@marceld23](https://github.com/marceld23)). Reports are handled privately and in
good faith.

Inspired by the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
